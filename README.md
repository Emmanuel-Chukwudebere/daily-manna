### 🍞 Daily Manna: Sudden Death Edition
An endless, high-stakes Bible verse memory game. What started as a serene, Sunday-school-style web app evolved into a fast-paced survival arcade game.
Test your scripture knowledge against a shrinking timer. Fast fingers build your score, but wrong answers will drain your clock.

## ⚡ The Mechanics
• Endless Survival: There is no final level. The game continues until your timer hits 0.0s.
• The Squeeze: You start with 20 seconds. Every verse you clear strips 1 second off your starting time for the next round, capping at a brutal 6-second minimum.
• Punishing Mistakes: Slotting the wrong word instantly deducts 3 seconds from your clock.
• Flex Your Score: Built-in integration with html2canvas allows players to instantly generate a beautiful, personalized scorecard to share on their WhatsApp status or social media.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Architecture
This project was built as an exercise in "vibe coding"—leveraging AI tools (Claude/Gemini Canvas) to bridge the gap between Product Design and Front-End Development with zero traditional coding background.
• Frontend: HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript
• Styling: Tailwind CSS (via CDN for a lightweight, zero-build-step setup)
• Image Generation: html2canvas for native share-sheet integration.
• Architecture: 100% contained in a single index.html file.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy
As a Product Designer, the goal was to take a traditionally "boring" concept (memorizing Bible verses) and apply modern mobile gaming psychology to it:
1. Dopamine Loops: Floating score popups and dynamic streak multipliers.
2. Frictionless UX: Tap-to-slot mechanics instead of clunky mobile drag-and-drop.
3. Visual Urgency: A pulsing, color-shifting timer bar and ambient background that reacts to the player's remaining time.
4. Viral Loops: Native mobile share-sheet integration to create competitive friction among friends.
